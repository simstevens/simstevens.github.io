We just updated the preprint to our paper on **"Branch-and-Cut for Mixed-Integer
Linear Decision-Dependent Robust Optimization"** (joint work with *Henri
Lefebvre*, *Martin Schmidt* and *Johannes Thürauf*). It is available right now
and can be found [here](https://optimization-online.org/?p=33886). The
accompanying open-source implementations are available
[here](https://github.com/simstevens/bnc-for-ddro).
